Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Scholar's Quest (the "app", "we", "us") collects, how it's used, and what choices you have. Scholar's Quest is a personal study planner and AI tutor. It has no traditional server backend — most of your data stays in your own browser.

1. Information We Collect

  • Google account info (if you sign in): your name, email address, and profile photo, provided by Google Identity Services when you sign in. We do not receive your Google password or access your Gmail, Drive, or other Google data.
  • Sign-in log: each time you sign in, your name, email, and the sign-in timestamp are recorded in a private Google Sheet controlled by the app owner, so they can see who has accessed the app and when.
  • App data (tasks, subjects, XP, theme, class/grade): stored only in your browser's local storage on your own device. It is not sent to any server we control, and we cannot see it.
  • AI Tutor conversations: if you use the AI Tutor feature, your messages (and some app context, like your subjects and progress) are sent directly from your browser to OpenRouter's API in order to generate a response. See Section 3.

2. How We Use Information

  • To let you sign in and access the app.
  • To let the app owner see who has used it and when (via the sign-in log).
  • To generate AI Tutor responses tailored to your subjects, level, and class/grade.

We do not sell your information, use it for advertising, or share it with any third party except the services listed in Section 3, which process it solely to provide the app's functionality.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Identity Services — handles the sign-in process. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sheets / Apps Script — stores the sign-in log described above, in a Sheet owned by the app owner.
  • OpenRouter — processes AI Tutor messages to generate responses. Governed by OpenRouter's Privacy Policy. Message content is sent to whichever underlying AI model OpenRouter routes the request to.

4. Data Storage & Retention

App data (tasks, XP, settings) lives in your browser's local storage indefinitely until you clear it yourself (via "Reset All Data" in Settings, or clearing your browser's site data). The sign-in log (name, email, timestamp) is retained in the app owner's Google Sheet until manually deleted.

5. Children's Privacy

Scholar's Quest is a study tool that may be used by students under 18. We do not knowingly collect more information from a child than is described above (name, email, and study progress), and we do not use this information for advertising. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information you'd like removed, contact us using the details in Section 8.

6. Your Choices

  • Sign out at any time using the "Sign out" button — this does not delete your sign-in log entry.
  • Clear local data using "Reset All Data" in Settings, or by clearing your browser's site storage for this app.
  • Request removal from the sign-in log by contacting the app owner (Section 8).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest revision.
